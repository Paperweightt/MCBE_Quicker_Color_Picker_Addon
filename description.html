Pick out blocks by their color and quickly make simple block palettes.

Perfect for:

  • Builders
  • Map makers
  • Creative players

Join Origami Studios discord for bugs, updates, and help!

Color Picker and Palette

Create a Palette

  • Select a starting point
  • Pick a side of the block (this will control how the palette with build)
  • Hold down the build/interact button
  • Move where you are looking to where you want the palette to end
  • Release the build/interact button

Pick a Color

  • Press build/interact on a block
  • Move the sliders by holding down build/interact
  • Press x to close the menu
